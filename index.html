My Top 3 Dungeons & Dragons Podcast!

by Charles R. Mitchell Jr.

Introduction

When it came to podcasts the only kinda i would listen to was sport based ones. Well all that changed
once the country had to shut down due to the pandemic. Sport podcasts became really stale and
repetitive. I had to find something else keep my mind busy while i was act work. So one day
went to Spotify and start searching for something new and thats when i discoverd the word of
Dungeons and Dragons. Below I have listed my top 3 favorite DnD podcasts to listen too.

Honorable Mentions

  • Join the Party

    Join the Party Podcast DM'd by Eric Silver is an autual play podcast involes around storytelling
    and having a good time. This was my very first DnD podcast that i listened and still enjoy it
    today. The show does a great job explaining the rules to newcomers as well as showing off new
    creative mechanics to keep long time players interested.

    Find them at:www.jointhepartypod.com

  • Dungeons and Daddies

    Dungeons and Daddies Podcast DM'd by Anthony Burch tells the story of four suburban dads from our
    world flung into a world of fantasy and magic in a quest to rescue their lost sons. If you are
    looking for a good laugh you have found the right podcast. This podcast focus around comedy and
    storytelling.

    Find them at:www.dungeonsanddaddies.com
